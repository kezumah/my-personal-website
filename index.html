About Me

I'm a graduate student at Northeastern University, where I'm pursuing a master's degree in computer science. Through my studies, I have developed a strong foundation in data structures, algorithms, and object-oriented design, enabling me to think about complex problems in simple, abstract terms.

Prior to my arrival at Northeastern, I graduated from Stony Brook University in 2017 with a bachelor's degree in civil engineering and worked for a few years in the heavy civil construction industry as a civil engineer.

Now, as a student technologist, I'm excited about applying my unique perspective and technical skills to building meaningful digital experiences. Currently, I'm learning web development and find myself fascinated by how tools and services built on the web simplify and enable large-scale information sharing and collaboration.
